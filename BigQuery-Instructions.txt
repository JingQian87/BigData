Instructions of BigQuery
Jing Qian (09/18/2019)

1. Build Project
2. Connect billing
3. Open BigQuery in console
4. Click project name under "Resources", "CREATE DATASET" (all default)
5. Click the dataset then "Create table"
6. Choose "Upload" to upload table, under "Schema" click "Schema and input parameters" to enable "Auto detect".
7. In the editor, input query.
8. If no warnings, click "Run" and check query results.
